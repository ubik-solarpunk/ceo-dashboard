💡 Top Insights

1. Activation Exceeds Discovery — Referral Loop? 🔄
92 activation events vs 74 discovery events. Users are activating (signing in, validating sessions) at a rate higher than new page views — suggesting return visits, direct links, or referral traffic bypassing the landing page. The product has organic pull.
2. Value Conversion Crisis — 1.4% 🚨
Only 1 value event (stream completed) from 74 discovery events. The gap between engagement (39 chat interactions) and value (1 completion) is massive. Users start chats but don't complete them — UX friction, unclear value prop, or AI quality issues.
3. Staging Drives 52% of All Events 🏗️
staging.theubik.com accounts for 140/270 events. The team is actively testing, but this inflates product metrics. Consider filtering staging from product analytics or tagging staging events separately.
